Confirm the tests give real confidence and close the few genuine gaps. Evidence-first result:
the suite is **strong**, 148 tests PASS (verified, both `unittest discover` and `pytest`), 80%
line coverage, 319 meaningful assertions vs only 5 bare `assertIsNotNone`, deterministic (no

narrow, high-value additions (pin an unpinned behavior, add a coverage signal), not
coverage-number chasing.
